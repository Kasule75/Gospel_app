Discover the Gospel

The Gospel Message

The Good News of Jesus Christ is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes.

What is the Gospel?

The word "Gospel" simply means "Good News". It is the message that God, the Creator of the universe, loves us and has made a way for us to be reconciled to Him through His Son, Jesus Christ. It is a message of hope, forgiveness, and eternal life.

Why We Need Salvation

The Bible teaches that "all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God" (Romans 3:23). Sin separates us from a holy God, and the result is spiritual death. We cannot bridge this gap by our own efforts, morality, or good works. We need a Savior.

God's Plan for You

God's plan is simple yet profound: "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life" (John 3:16). By trusting in Jesus alone for your salvation, you are forgiven and adopted into God's family forever.

About Jesus

He is the image of the invisible God, the firstborn of all creation.

Who is Jesus?

Jesus Christ is the Son of God, the second person of the Trinity. He is fully God and fully man, who came to earth to reveal God to us and reconcile us to Him.

His Life and Teachings

Jesus lived a sinless life, teaching love, repentance, and the Kingdom of God. His teachings, found in the Gospels, challenge us to love our neighbors and God above all.

The core of our faith is that Jesus died for our sins and rose again on the third day, conquering death and offering eternal life to all who believe in Him.

How to Read the Bible

Start with the Gospels (Matthew, Mark, Luke, John) to learn about Jesus. Pray for understanding before you read, and try to read a little every day.

Bible Reading Plans

Follow a structured plan to read through the Bible in a year, or focus on specific topics like peace, forgiveness, or wisdom. Consistency is key.

Statement of Faith

The core beliefs that guide our ministry and life.

We believe in one God, existing eternally in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and in His personal return in power and glory.

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, the only infallible, authoritative Word of God.

We believe that for the salvation of lost and sinful people, regeneration by the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential.

Questions of Faith

Is Jesus the only way to God?

Jesus said, "I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me." (John 14:6).

How can I know God exists?

God reveals Himself through creation, conscience, and His Word.

What if I've made big mistakes?

God's grace is greater than any sin. "If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just and will forgive us our sins..." (1 John 1:9).

How do I know I'm truly saved?

Salvation is a gift of grace through faith, not works. (Ephesians 2:8-9).
